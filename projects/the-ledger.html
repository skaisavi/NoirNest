Product / Fintech

The Ledger

A finance product interface that trades noise for calm, helping users understand money movement at a glance.

ClientThe Ledger
ScopeUX audit, UI system, prototype
Timeline6 weeks

Challenge

Turn dense financial workflows into a product people trust quickly.

The Ledger team had strong functionality but too many competing UI patterns. Users needed better hierarchy, clearer status language, and flows that made account activity feel transparent instead of intimidating.

NoirNest rebuilt the core dashboard, transaction review, onboarding, and alert patterns into a compact interface system that could scale with the product team.

Signal Hierarchy

Critical balances, movement, and risk alerts were organized into fast scanning zones.

Trust Patterns

Microcopy, confirmation states, and history details reduced uncertainty in high-stakes actions.

Component Library

A lean component set gave product and engineering a shared language for future features.
