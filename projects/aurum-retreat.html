Hospitality / Web

Aurum Retreat

An immersive hospitality site and booking path for a private coastal resort with a deliberately slower pace.

ClientAurum Retreat
ScopeWeb strategy, UI, booking UX
Timeline10 weeks

Challenge

Build desire without making the booking journey feel hidden.

Aurum Retreat needed a digital experience that could sell atmosphere, room categories, seasonal packages, and private enquiries without becoming a generic resort brochure.

NoirNest created an editorial site structure with immersive imagery, clear availability prompts, and a booking flow that respected the brand's private, appointment-led tone.

Editorial Pace

Long-form destination storytelling was paired with concise decision points throughout the journey.

Room Discovery

Suite pages balanced practical detail with image-led atmosphere and comparison-friendly specs.

Private Enquiry

The booking path supported both direct reservations and high-touch concierge requests.
